Grant-A-Request

About

Grant-A-Request® grants life-changing requests for elderly people with critical illnesses. We seek to bring every eligible elder’s request to life because every old-timer deserves a second-childhood. Grant-A-Request has become the world’s leading elders’s request-granting organization, serving elders in nearly 50 countries worldwide. Together, generous donors, supporters, staff and more than 43,000 volunteers unite to deliver hope to request-elders and their families when they need it most. Please join us by making your donation to help us grant more life-changing requests come true!

Stay Informed

For elders diagnosed with critical illnesses, a request granted can be one the happiest moments in their lives. A request can be that spark that helps the elderly believe that anything is still possible and gives them the strength to fight harder against their illnesses. This impact is why we are driven to grant every one of these request.
